Cooper Bespoke Conversions — assets
====================================

Drop real photos in here and the site picks them up automatically.

BEFORE / AFTER SLIDER (the "See the transformation" section)
  before.jpg   — an empty/bare panel van (interior or exterior)
  after.jpg    — the same (or a representative) finished Cooper build
  As soon as both files exist, the styled placeholders disappear and the
  draggable slider shows your real photos. Landscape ~16:9 works best.

SOCIAL SHARE IMAGE
  og-image.png — 1200x630 image shown when the site is shared on
  WhatsApp / Facebook / LinkedIn / X / iMessage. A generated hero image
  is in place; replace with a real photo of a finished build when ready
  (keep it 1200x630).
