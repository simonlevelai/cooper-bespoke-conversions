What we do

Everything your conversion needs, under one roof

No subcontracting your dream out to five different trades. Design, joinery, electrics and finish all happen in our workshop.

Full van conversions

Start to finish builds on Sprinters, Crafters, Transits and VWs — from empty shell to road-ready home.

Bespoke cabinetry

Custom joinery and furniture designed for your layout — every drawer, bed and worktop made to measure.

Electrics & off-grid power

Solar, leisure batteries, inverters and 12V systems wired safely so you can park up anywhere and stay powered.

Heating, water & plumbing

Diesel heaters, fresh and grey water, sinks and showers — comfort that works through a British winter.

Upholstery & soft finish

Cushions, seating and trim in fabrics you choose — the difference between a van and somewhere you want to be.

Upgrades & servicing

Already converted? We refit, upgrade and put right existing builds — pop-tops, layouts, electrics and more.

About Cooper

Built by people who actually live the life

Cooper Bespoke Conversions is a workshop-based campervan builder for people who want something genuinely their own — not an off-the-shelf kit dropped into a van.

Every conversion is designed around one customer, one van and one way of travelling. We sweat the details most people never see: how the weight sits, how the wiring is run, how a drawer feels when you pull it open on a wet night three years from now.

The result is a van that's safe, beautiful and built to keep going — wherever the road takes it.
